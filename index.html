The Pacific Frontline: Mapping Coastal Risk

Coastal and Elevation Risk

Interactive Population View

This dashboard visualizes population data around coasts for key Pacific island nations in bands of 1 Kilometer, 5 kilometers, and 10 Kilometers. While the coast is central to Pacific identity and economy, it’s also where climate change hits hardest—making it essential to understand who lives there and how close they are to the rising sea.
